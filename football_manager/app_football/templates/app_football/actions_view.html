Bulik's Football Manager

On the top you can see black colored NAVBAR. There are many usefull informations like so:

TEAM

- This is the place where you can find all details about your Players

LEAGUE TABLE

- Shows actual position of your team in confrontation with another players

SCHEDULE

- This is the place where you can find information about rounds of matches.

TRAINING

- Place where you can train your players, make their skills grow bigger to help you win.

- PERSONAL TRAINING costs you 2 points of ENERGY and you can choose Player and his attribute you want to train

- TEAM TRAINING costs you 4 points of ENERGY and your 3 random players gets 2 points of Attack or Defence

MATCH DAY!

- The most important thing! There you can play your matches. Each match costs 6 points of ENERGY

ENERGY

- This is the information about your actual energy level. It regenerates every 1 minute

- Actually logged user
